About Us

Our Orign Story

After hearing about the professional roadblocks so many face, our CEO Manish Karani had a vision to create a safe space for everyone who needs it; a virtual portal powered by ASK Consulting with access to expert resources including upskilling and reskilling tools, resume writing workshops, a personal job concierge and more. We’re a guide for both those looking for work and those looking to hire talented individuals for their organization.

Who We Are

Incredible Me! Powered by Equity is a career launching resource for, well, everyone.

Everyone who ever needed mentorship but didn’t or couldn’t get it. Everyone whose ever been told no. Everyone whose ever believed they could, but life got in the way. We want to see you succeed no matter your economic status, race or creed...or anything else for that matter.

When we say everyone, we mean it.

Why? Because creating a more inclusive, diverse and equitable workforce is our goal. We prepare you for a career and help you master the latest technologies and professional skills. We add you to our cloud of diverse candidates and help organizations transform their workplace with incredible people like you.
